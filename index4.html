Filipino Food I Love

Adobo

Adobo is a popular Filipino dish made of meat, typically chicken or pork, marinated in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and various spices. It is rich in flavor and often served with rice.

Pork Sisig

Pork Sisig is a savory, spicy Filipino dish made from pork parts such as ears, cheeks, and snout. It is usually served on a sizzling plate and topped with egg and chili peppers.

Sinigang

Sinigang is a tangy, savory soup made with a variety of meats like pork, shrimp, or fish, and vegetables like kangkong (water spinach) and radish, cooked with tamarind for that signature sour taste.

Pastil

Pastil is a Filipino rice meal consisting of rice topped with marinated chicken or beef, wrapped in a banana leaf. It is often served as a snack or lunch and is known for its delicious and savory flavor.
